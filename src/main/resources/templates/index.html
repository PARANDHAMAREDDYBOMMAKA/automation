How to Get Your Cookies

  1. Open your browser and go to kalvium.community
  2. Log in with your Google account
  3. Press F12 or right-click → Inspect to open DevTools
  4. Go to the Application tab
  5. Under Storage → Cookies, select kalvium.community
  6. Copy the values of: AUTH_SESSION_ID, KEYCLOAK_IDENTITY, and KEYCLOAK_SESSION
  7. Paste them in the form above and click Save
